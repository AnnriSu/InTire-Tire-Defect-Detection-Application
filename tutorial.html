InTIRE video tutorial

InTIRE video tutorial

Step 1: Open the App

Launch the inTIRE app on your device. You will be directed to the main camera screen.

Step 2: Position the Camera

Point your phone's camera directly at the tire. Make sure the damaged area (e.g., cracks, worn tread, or puncture) is clearly visible and well-lit.

Step 3: Automatic Scanning

Hold your phone steady while the app automatically scans the tire. No need to press any button—just wait a few seconds for the detection to complete.

Step 4: View Results

Once scanning is finished, the app will display the detected issue, its severity level, and recommended actions (e.g., repair or replace).

Step 5: Save or Mark as Resolved

Tap "Save" to record the inspection. If the issue has already been fixed, you may mark it as "Resolved" to update your dashboard.
