InTire Privacy Policy

Last updated March 01, 2026

Welcome to InTire: A Mobile Application for Tire defect inspection using Deep Learning and CNN-Powered Image Detection. By creating an account and using this application, you agree to comply with the following Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully before using the app.

Information We Collect

Personal Information
When creating an account, the app may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Vehicle Information (optional)


Device Information
The app may collect limited technical data such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App usage statistics


Camera Data
The app uses your device camera to capture tire images for inspection. Captured images may be stored temporarily or saved in inspection reports.

How We Use Your Information
The collected information is used to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Perform tire inspections using camera analysis
  • Save inspection history and reports
  • Improve application performance and accuracy
  • Provide technical support and updates


Data Storage and Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no digital system can guarantee 100% security.

Sharing of Information
We do not sell, rent, or trade personal user information to third parties. Information may only be shared if required by law or legal authorities.

User Rights
Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal information
  • Update or correct account details
  • Request deletion of their account and data


Camera Permission
The application requires camera access to capture tire images for inspection. Camera data is used only for inspection purposes and will not be accessed without user permission.

Data Retention
Inspection records and user data may be stored for as long as the user account remains active unless the user requests deletion.

Changes to Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant updates within the application.

Contact Us
If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the application support team.
